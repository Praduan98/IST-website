OPENING NARRATIVE
Every GTM system is only as good as the signals it runs on. If you can't detect intent, you can't act on it. If you detect it but can't enrich it, you don't know who to reach. If you enrich it but can't score it, everything looks equally important. And if you score it but can't activate it, the signal dies in a spreadsheet. We build that layer — so every signal flows from detection to enrichment to scoring to activation, automatically.

OUR APPROACH TO SIGNAL ARCHITECTURE
 
Signal Source Mapping
We identify every relevant signal source for your business — website visitors, hiring data, funding events, tech adoption, social conversations, product usage, ad engagement. Then we connect them into one unified detection layer.
 
Enrichment Pipeline
Raw signals are useless without context. We build enrichment pipelines that attach firmographic, technographic, and contact data to every signal — so you know who, what company, what role, and why now.
 
Signal Scoring Engine
Not every signal is worth acting on. We build scoring models that rank signals by intent strength, ICP fit, timing, and engagement recency — so your team focuses on the signals that convert.
 
Activation Triggers
Scored signals automatically trigger outreach, ads, CRM updates, and internal alerts. No manual review. No delays. The right action happens the moment the signal fires.
