OUR APPROACH TO THE DARK LOOP™
 
D — Detect
Identify deep intent from hiring activity, funding rounds, website behavior, content engagement, product usage, tech installs, and social conversations. We monitor 40+ signal types across your total addressable market.
 
A — Augment
Enrich every signal with buyer role, company stage, firmographics, technographics, and engagement context. Raw signals become rich buyer profiles ready for personalized engagement.
 
R — Reach
AI agents run multi-channel outreach — email, LinkedIn, and ads — triggered by enriched signals. Hyper-personalized messaging at scale. Meetings booked automatically.
 
K — Kaizen
Continuous optimization based on response rates, ICP match quality, conversion data, and pipeline outcomes. The system gets smarter every cycle. Compounding GTM performance.
