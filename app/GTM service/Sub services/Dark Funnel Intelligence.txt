OUR APPROACH TO DARK FUNNEL INTELLIGENCE
 
Anonymous Visitor Identification
Thousands visit your site without converting. Using reverse IP resolution, device fingerprinting, and identity tools, we identify the company, the behavior, and the pages visited — then trigger targeted follow-up.
 
Hiring & Funding Signal Detection
Job postings and funding rounds are buying signals hiding in plain sight. We monitor them in real time and activate outreach the moment they appear.
 
Competitor & Social Listening
Buyers discuss problems publicly before they search for solutions. We capture competitor complaints, feature requests, and buying questions from LinkedIn, G2, Reddit, and industry forums.
 
Tech Adoption & Churn Signals
When prospects adopt complementary tools, upgrade platforms, or churn off competitors — that’s a buying window. We detect these technographic signals and act on them instantly.
 
Product Usage Intelligence
For SaaS companies, your own product data is a massive intent engine. Free users hitting paid limits, trial users showing activation, inactive users re-engaging — we capture these signals and route them to sales.
