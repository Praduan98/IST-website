SECTION 8 — FAQs
Frequently Asked Questions
What is GTM Engineering?
GTM Engineering replaces traditional sales and marketing with systems — signal detection, AI agents, CRM automation, and performance ads all working together. Instead of hoping leads come in, you engineer a system that finds and engages buyers automatically.
What is the dark funnel?
The dark funnel is the 98% of buying activity that happens outside your CRM — anonymous website visits, job postings, funding rounds, competitor research, social conversations. We build systems that detect these signals and turn them into pipeline.
How is this different from hiring more SDRs?
SDRs can’t personalize for every signal, every buyer, and every context. AI agents can. They run 24/7, never miss a signal, and scale without adding headcount. Your SDRs focus on closing.
Which tools do you use?
Clay, RB2B, Factors.ai, CommonRoom for signals. Apollo, Smartlead, Instantly, Lemlist for outreach. HubSpot/Salesforce for CRM. GPT-4o and Claude for AI agents. LinkedIn, Google, Meta for ads.
How fast can we see results?
Most GTM engines go live in 4–6 weeks. Signal detection and outreach can start generating pipeline within the first 2 weeks.
Do you work with our existing CRM?
Yes. We integrate into HubSpot, Salesforce, or whatever you use. No ripping and replacing.
