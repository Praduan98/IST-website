OUR APPROACH TO RB2B SERVICES
 
Person-Level Visitor Identification
RB2B identifies the actual person visiting your site — name, job title, company, LinkedIn URL, and the pages they viewed. Not just IP-to-company mapping. Real people.
	 
CRM Integration & Lead Creation
Identified visitors automatically create or update contacts in HubSpot or Salesforce with full context — pages visited, time on site, visit frequency, and company data.
	 
Signal-Triggered Outreach
High-intent page visits — pricing, case studies, comparison pages — automatically trigger personalized outreach sequences via email or LinkedIn. The follow-up happens while they’re still interested.
	 
Lead Scoring Enhancement
RB2B visitor data feeds your lead scoring model. A contact who visited your pricing page three times this week scores differently than one who read a blog post once.
	 
Retargeting Audience Sync
Identified visitors pushed to LinkedIn and Google ad audiences for precision retargeting. Ads shown to people who already showed intent on your site.

FAQs
Is RB2B GDPR compliant?
RB2B focuses on US-based identification. For international traffic, we configure compliance settings and recommend complementary tools for other regions.
How is RB2B different from Leadfeeder?
Leadfeeder identifies companies. RB2B identifies individuals — names, roles, LinkedIn profiles. Person-level is far more actionable.
How fast can RB2B be deployed?
Most deployments go live within 1 week including CRM integration and outreach triggers.
