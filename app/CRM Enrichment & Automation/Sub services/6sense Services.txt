OUR APPROACH TO 6SENSE SERVICES
 
Account Intent Detection
6sense tracks account-level research activity across the web — topics searched, competitors compared, categories explored. We configure the intent topics and segments that matter to your business.
	 
Buying Stage Prediction
6sense’s AI predicts where each account sits in the buying journey — awareness, consideration, decision, purchase. We connect these predictions to your CRM and outreach so timing matches intent.
	 
CRM & Workflow Integration
Intent data, buying stages, and account scores pushed directly into HubSpot or Salesforce. Lifecycle stages updated. ABM plays triggered. Sales reps alerted — all automatically.
	 
ABM Ad Activation
6sense audience segments synced to LinkedIn and Google for account-based advertising. Ads shown only to accounts in active buying stages. No wasted spend on unaware accounts.
	 
Revenue AI & Analytics
6sense’s predictive models connected to your pipeline reporting. Know which accounts are most likely to close, where to focus resources, and which campaigns drive real revenue.
