CUSTOM GPT APP STRATEGY AND EXECUTION
We begin by understanding what your team actually needs from AI
The tasks, the data, and the decisions it should support. We then design the model strategy, deciding which LLMs handle which tasks and how they’ll be orchestrated.
We connect to your company data using retrieval pipelines 
Grounding every AI response in your documents, knowledge base, and SOPs. This means your GPT app gives answers specific to your business, not the internet.
Beyond the core build, we implement smart model routing 
So your team always gets the best output at the lowest cost. Our apps ship with React or Flutter on the frontend and Node or Django on the backend, with LangChain handling orchestration. Monitoring tracks which models perform best — so the app gets smarter over time.
