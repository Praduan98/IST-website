Frequently Asked Questions
How long does it take to build a custom AI product?
Most projects ship in 3–6 weeks. Simpler apps like chatbots can be faster. Complex AI SaaS platforms take 8–12 weeks. Either way, we move fast.
Which AI models do you work with?
GPT-4o, Claude, Gemini, Llama, Cohere, HuggingFace — and any open-source model that fits. We pick the right model for the job, not the most expensive one.
Can you build mobile apps?
Yes. We use Flutter for cross-platform mobile and React/Next.js for web. One codebase, all platforms.
Can you integrate AI into our existing tools?
Yes. We plug AI into HubSpot, Salesforce, Slack, Notion, Airtable, your database — whatever your team uses. No need to replace anything.
What happens after you ship?
We provide ongoing support, monitoring, optimization, and new feature development. Your AI product keeps getting better over time.
Is our data safe?
Yes. We follow enterprise-grade security practices. Your data stays in your infrastructure. We don’t cut corners on security.
