OPENING NARRATIVE
Your ad platforms and your CRM live in different worlds. LinkedIn doesn’t know which of your leads became SQLs. Google can’t see which keywords generated closed deals. HubSpot shows leads as “Direct” because UTM data breaks between click and CRM. Your marketing team reports on CPL. Your sales team reports on pipeline. Nobody agrees on what’s working. CRM and ads integration closes this loop — connecting ad spend to CRM data to pipeline to revenue.

OUR APPROACH TO CRM & ADS INTEGRATION
 
Audience Sync
CRM contact lists, account lists, and lifecycle segments synced to LinkedIn, Google, and Meta as customer match audiences. Your ads target exactly who your CRM says they should.
	 
Offline Conversion Pipeline
CRM stage changes — MQL, SQL, opportunity, closed-won — synced back to ad platforms as offline conversions. Algorithms optimize for pipeline, not form fills.
	 
Revenue Attribution
Full-journey attribution from first ad click to closed deal. Know which campaigns, keywords, and creatives actually generate revenue — not just leads.
	 
Lifecycle-Based Retargeting
CRM lifecycle stages trigger ad targeting changes. Leads see nurture ads. MQLs see conversion ads. Customers see expansion ads. Each stage gets the right message.
	 
Unified Dashboards
One dashboard showing ad spend, CRM pipeline, and revenue attribution together. Marketing and sales looking at the same numbers.
	 
OUR PHILOSOPHY
 
Front
Close the Loop
Ads without CRM connection optimize for the wrong thing.
When ad platforms see pipeline data, they find better prospects — automatically.
Back
Ad platform algorithms are powerful — but they optimize based on the signals you give them. If you only report form fills, the algorithm finds more form fillers. When you feed back MQL, SQL, and closed-won data as offline conversions, the algorithm learns what a real buyer looks like and optimizes accordingly.
Front
One Truth
Marketing reports CPL. Sales reports pipeline. Leadership sees conflicting numbers.
When CRM and ads share one attribution model, every team agrees on what’s working.
Back
The most damaging gap in B2B is the disconnect between marketing metrics and sales metrics. Integration gives both teams the same attribution data — which campaigns drive pipeline, which drive revenue, and which waste money. Arguments end. Optimization begins.
Front
Dynamic Audiences
Static ad audiences decay. CRM-synced audiences stay current.
When your ad targeting updates automatically from CRM data, every impression reaches the right person.
Back
Uploading CSV lists to ad platforms is slow, error-prone, and always outdated. CRM-synced audiences update automatically — new leads get targeted, closed customers get excluded, lifecycle changes trigger audience shifts. Your ads always target the right people.
Front
Full-Journey Visibility
From first impression to closed deal in one view.
That’s how you know which ad dollars actually generate revenue.
Back
Most companies can tell you their CPL but not their cost-per-closed-deal by campaign. Full CRM integration gives you that visibility — from first ad impression through CRM stages to closed revenue. That’s the data that changes budget allocation.
