OUR APPROACH TO BRIDGE ADS
 
Product Explainers
15–30 second clarity videos that explain what your product does, who it’s for, and why it matters — without selling. Built for feed consumption, not long-form attention.
	 
Founder POV Ads
Your founder or leader shares a perspective on an industry problem. Authentic, opinion-led, trust-building. The highest-engagement ad format in B2B.
	 
Micro Case Studies
30-second visual case studies showing real results — the problem, the solution, the outcome. Social proof that fits in an ad slot.
	 
Clarity Videos
Motion graphics and animated explainers that break down complex products or processes into simple, visual narratives. Built for LinkedIn and YouTube.
	 
Retargeting Pool Expansion
Bridge Ads are designed to maximize engagement and video views — building massive retargeting pools of educated, warmed audiences ready for conversion campaigns.
