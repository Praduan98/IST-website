OUR APPROACH TO SIGNAL-LED ABM CAMPAIGNS
 
Signal-Based Account Selection
We build dynamic account lists based on real-time buying signals — job postings, funding events, technology adoption, website visits, G2 research, and CRM lifecycle changes. Lists update automatically as signals change.
	 
Buying Committee Targeting
Ads reach the entire buying committee — not just one contact. Decision-makers, influencers, and users at target accounts see coordinated messaging across LinkedIn, Google, and Meta.
	 
CRM-Synced Audiences
Account lists and contact data from HubSpot or Salesforce synced to ad platforms as customer match audiences. Ads always target your latest, most relevant accounts.
	 
Multi-Touch ABM Journeys
Coordinated ad sequences that move accounts through awareness, consideration, and decision stages. Bridge Ads warm. Conversion ads close. Retargeting reinforces.
	 
Pipeline Attribution
Every ABM ad campaign traced from impression to CRM to closed deal. Know which signals, accounts, and creatives actually generate revenue.
	 
OUR PHILOSOPHY
 
Front
Targeted ABM
Traditional ABM targets static lists. Signal-led ABM targets real-time intent.
When ads follow signals, every dollar reaches an account that’s actually buying.
Back
Static ABM lists decay fast. Companies change, priorities shift, and buying windows close. Signal-led ABM dynamically updates targeting based on real-time intent — job changes, funding, website behavior, tech adoption. Your ads always reach accounts in active buying mode.
Front
Soft Outreach
Ads are a soft outreach channel alongside email and LinkedIn.
When a target account sees your Bridge Ad, reads your email, and gets your LinkedIn message — that’s multi-channel ABM done right.
Back
Think of ABM ads as a soft outreach channel. Email and LinkedIn are direct. Ads are ambient — they build familiarity, trust, and awareness without being intrusive. When all three channels coordinate, the combined effect is significantly stronger than any single channel.
Front
Precision, Not Spray
Every ad dollar should reach an account that matters.
Customer match audiences from CRM and signal data ensure zero waste on non-ICP accounts.
Back
We build customer match audiences directly from your CRM and signal data. No broad firmographic targeting. No hoping the platform’s algorithm figures it out. Every impression reaches a decision-maker at an account you’ve identified as in-market.
Front
Closed-Loop Measurement
If you can’t trace an ad impression to a closed deal, you’re guessing.
Full pipeline attribution from ad to CRM to revenue. That’s how you know what works.
Back
We connect every ABM campaign to your CRM so you can trace the full journey — which signal triggered the targeting, which creative generated the engagement, which account progressed to opportunity, and which deal closed. That’s real ABM measurement.
