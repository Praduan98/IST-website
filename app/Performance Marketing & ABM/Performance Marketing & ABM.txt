Ads Aren’t the Issue. The System Is.
Most B2B ad programs fail after the click — because the funnel, tracking, and signals aren’t connected. Cold ads hit buyers with zero context or trust. There’s no middle funnel warming them up before the demo ask. GA4 and Tag Manager are misconfigured or incomplete. Ads aren’t connected to HubSpot or your CRM. Attribution is broken — half your leads show as “Direct.” And your agency uses e-commerce tactics on B2B audiences.
