Why Insightstap
 
Company Culture
Insightstap isn’t a traditional agency. We don’t run campaigns and call it a day. We engineer revenue systems — signal detection pipelines, AI-powered outreach, CRM automation, Bridge Ads, and full-funnel analytics. Our culture reflects that. We’re builders. We ship. We measure impact. And we celebrate when our systems generate real pipelines for real companies.
 
We’re remote-first and globally distributed. That means no commutes, no mandatory office hours, and no micromanagement. What we do have: clear goals, fast feedback loops, direct access to leadership, and the autonomy to do your best work from wherever you work best.
 
Team Values
Everything we do runs on four values. They’re not motivational posters. They’re how we hire, evaluate, and make decisions every day.
 
Own It
Take ownership of your work, your outcomes, and your growth. We don’t chase people for updates. We trust our team to set the bar high and clear it. Ownership inspires initiative, and initiative drives results.
 
Ship It
Ideas are cheap. Execution matters. We value people who move fast, iterate, and get things into the world. Done and improving beats perfect and stuck in a deck.
 
Measure It
Every system we build is measured by its impact on pipeline and revenue. We apply the same standard to ourselves. If it doesn’t move the needle, we change the approach.
 
Have Fun
We genuinely enjoy what we do. Building AI agents, engineering signal systems, creating Bridge Ads that outperform cold demo campaigns — this is exciting work. When you love the work, you naturally do it better.

Our Recruitment Process[d]
Our hiring process is designed to be fast, fair, and transparent. We respect your time and ensure you have clarity at every step. Most hiring processes are completed within 2 weeks—from the first screen to the final offer.
________________


Step 1 — Application Submission
Apply through our internal careers page or trusted job portals . Submit your resume along with a brief note on why you’d like to join Insightstap. This helps us understand your interest, experience, and alignment with the role.
________________


Step 2 — Application Review
Our team carefully reviews your application to assess your experience, achievements, and fit for the role. We typically respond within 3–5 business days.
________________


Step 3 — Initial Screen
A 30-minute conversation with our People & Culture team. We’ll discuss your background, career aspirations, and answer your questions about the role and company. This is a two-way conversation—we encourage you to evaluate us as much as we evaluate you.
________________


Step 4 — XOBIN Assessment
A role-specific skills assessment powered by XOBIN. This is a practical evaluation of real-world skills relevant to your role—whether it’s a technical challenge, GTM scenario, or a marketing exercise.
________________


Step 5 — Final Interview
A discussion with the hiring manager and potential team members. We’ll review your assessment, explore your experience in depth, and understand how you approach real challenges. You’ll also get a chance to meet the people you’ll be working with.
________________


Step 6 — Documentation & Background Verification (BGV)
Post final discussions, we initiate the documentation process and background verification. This includes verification of your previous employment, education, and other relevant details to ensure a smooth and compliant onboarding experience.
________________


Step 7 — Offer & Onboarding
If selected, you’ll receive a clear and transparent offer within 2–3 business days. Compensation, benefits, and start date are all clearly outlined. Our onboarding includes a structured first-week plan, a dedicated buddy, and defined 30/60/90-day goals so you can start strong and succeed from day one.

Frequently Asked Questions
 
Can I apply for multiple roles at the same time?
Yes, absolutely. We recommend you consider your skills and interests and limit your applications to no more than 2–3 roles. After the initial screen, our team can help align you with the best opportunity for your skills and career goals.
What does the XOBIN assessment look like?
It’s a practical, role-specific skills evaluation. Engineering roles get technical challenges. Strategy roles get GTM scenarios. Marketing roles get content or campaign exercises. It’s designed to reflect real work, not trick questions.
How long does the hiring process take?
Most processes complete within 2 weeks from initial screen to offer. We respect your time and move quickly when we find the right person.
Do you offer internships?
Yes. Paid internships running 3–6 months in Engineering, GTM Strategy, Marketing, and Design. 
What if I don’t see a role that fits?
Send your resume and a note to careers@insightstap.com. Tell us what you’d build. We’re always open to exceptional people, even if there’s no open listing.
